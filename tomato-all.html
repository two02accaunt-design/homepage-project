Agridera Fresh Market Tomato Hybrids

Agridera aims to be one of the world’s leading companies in the breeding, production and marketing of tomato seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world.

Our tomato portfolio offers hybrids for open-field and greenhouse cultivation, focusing on yield, disease resistance, fruit quality and long shelf life.

Greenhouse Tomato Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. We develop round and beef tomatoes as well as special varieties.

Open-field Indeterminate Tomatoes

This breeding is for Brazil, Morocco and Mediterranean regions. The focus is on round and elongated shapes with high yield.

Open-field Bush Type Tomatoes

This breeding is for Mediterranean countries. We develop hybrids for processing and fresh market.

Agridera operates through international and local distributors around the world.
